About Lunex

Lunex is pioneering the future of space exploration by building a permanent and sustainable human presence on the Moon. Our mission is to advance science, fuel innovation, and inspire humanity to reach beyond Earth. With cutting-edge technology, global partnerships, and a relentless spirit of discovery, Lunex is lighting the path for future generations of explorers.

Our Mission
